# ChemMon Reports (Dashboards, National Reports, EU Annual Reports)

<!-- Source: EFSA Supporting Publications - 2026 -  - Chemical monitoring reporting guidance  2026 data collection.pdf, Section 10 / Table 13 -->

## Overview

EFSA uses ChemMon submissions for scientific assessments and produces a series of reports derived from each calendar year of data. The guidance summarises these reports and their inclusion filters in Table 13. (ChemMon 2026 p149-151)

These filters rely heavily on the per-record **domain flags** described in [[reporting-flags]]. (ChemMon 2026 p150)

## Validation dashboard reports (for data providers and validators)

<!-- Source: ChemMon 2026 p150 (Table 13) -->

- Reports exist for: pesticides, VMPR, chemical contaminants, food additives, and food flavourings.
- Only records with the relevant domain flag value **= 1** appear in each section of the validation reports.
- A submitted record may appear in more than one section of the validation dashboard report.
- Dashboards with summarised/aggregated results are generated by EFSA per reporting organisation within a country. (ChemMon 2026 p150)

## National reports (as supporting tools during validation)

<!-- Source: ChemMon 2026 p150 (Table 13) -->

For contaminants/additives/flavourings, the guidance describes national reports generated per reporting organisation, including:

- Contaminants: all records with `is_occ=1`
- Food additives: all records with `is_add=1`
- Food flavourings: all records with `is_flav=1` (ChemMon 2026 p150)

Each submitted record may appear in more than one domain’s national report. (ChemMon 2026 p150)

## National reports for delivery to the European Commission (VMPR)

<!-- Source: ChemMon 2026 p150 (Table 13) -->

- VMPR national reports are generated only for the VMPR results domain: all records with `is_vmpr=1`.
- Reports are generated per reporting organisation within a country.
- The guidance notes the VMPR national report can be created any time by the data providers/national organisation. (ChemMon 2026 p150)

## Statutory EU Annual Reports (pesticides, VMPR)

<!-- Source: ChemMon 2026 p151 (Table 13) -->

The guidance describes statutory EU annual reports generated only for pesticides and VMPR results domains, and explicitly calls out the VMPR filter as:

- all records with `is_vmpr=1`, or
- all records with `is_vmpr=1` excluding VMPR matrix class = "Other". (ChemMon 2026 p151)

## Aggregated extraction for EC Annual Report on Official Control (AROC)

<!-- Source: ChemMon 2026 p151 (Table 13) -->

The guidance describes an aggregated extraction used to pre-fill a pre-defined AROC table (Table 1.4), covering official-control data from four ChemMon domains:

- pesticides, VMPR, contaminants, and food additives. (ChemMon 2026 p151)

This extraction is coupled with the EC AROC platform to support MANCP annual reporting. A submitted record/sample may appear in more than one EC AROC domain. (ChemMon 2026 p151)
